Hamburger

Description

The best burger recipe for summertime grilling! This juicy burger is jam-packed with all kinds of stuff and has no tasteless bread crumbs. Serve on buns with your favorite condiments.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill
  2. Combine meat with other ingredients
  3. Cook patties about 4 to 5 minutes per side
