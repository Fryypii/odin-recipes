Rice

Description

This is a foolproof recipe for cooking Jollof rice, West Africa's most popular dish. I like to serve it as a side with my West African-style grilled beef (Suya), but it can also be a main course.

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Rice
  • Pepper
  • Chili
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Onion
  • Paprika
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Make the sauce
  2. Add ingredients and sauce to pan
  3. Cook rice
  4. Mix everything
  5. Cook for 5 more minutes
